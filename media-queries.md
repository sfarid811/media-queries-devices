## Device orientation
- To add landscape or portrait orientation, use the following code:

### For portrait
- and (orientation : portrait) 

### For landscape:
and (orientation : landscape) 

## iPhone 12 Mini
@media only screen 
    and (device-width: 375px) 
    and (device-height: 812px) 
    and (-webkit-device-pixel-ratio: 3) { }
